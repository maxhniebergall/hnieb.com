Information Collection and Use

The Pizza Value Chrome Extension is designed to analyze web pages to identify pizza deals. It operates locally within your browser.

Data Handling

  • The extension does not track your browsing history.
  • It only activates on specific, supported restaurant domains.
  • No personal data is sent to external servers.
